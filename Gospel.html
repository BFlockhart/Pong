Laws of the Realm


The following rules are to be enforced by the nominated umpire. Punishments will typically be in the form of a shot being discounted or a cup to be drunk depending on the severity.


  • Elbows behind the table's edge.
  • Trick shots allowed if you regain the ball after taking your shot.
  • Trick shots must be respectable (fully sick).
  • If both people sink in the same cup then it is splash and thus +2.
  • Rethrows if both teammates sink a cup.
  • Bouncer is 2 cups, but can be swatted away after the bounce.
  • Bouncing off a cup into another cup only counts as 1.
  • No interfering with a ball that has been thrown unless it bounces.
  • Redemption is to be played after last cup is sunk, unless a team performs Coup de Grace.
  • Coupe de Grace is when both teammates sink in the same cup.
  • Intentionally pegging the ball to knock off the cup/s is illegal.
  • Cups nearing the edge may be moved from the edge by a thumb knuckle.
  • Liquid in cups must be to decided regulation.
  • Cups must be drunk immediately after they are sunk.
